## How the scorecard works

Score any player 1–3 on each of four dimensions. The spreadsheet calculates a weighted composite score and returns a recommendation tier.

**Age** (weight: 25%) — scored by draft age relative to historical comps. A 19-year-old #1 pick scores 3. A 23-year-old scores 1.

**Draft Pedigree** (weight: 30%) — top-3 picks score 3, picks 4–10 score 2, picks 11+ score 1. For undrafted players, a special evaluation rubric applies.

**Persona** (weight: 25%) — media presence, cultural crossover signals, nickname recognition, and early sponsorship activity. Scored with a guided rubric including yes/no qualifier questions.

**Winning** (weight: 20%) — championship pedigree at any level: high school nationals, major conference NCAA title, international gold medals. Team winning culture also scored.

**Historical calibration tab:** The spreadsheet includes pre-scored profiles for LeBron James (2003), Patrick Mahomes (2017), Victor Wembanyama (2023), Luka Doncic (2018), and 10 other players.
