## What the dashboard does

**Input:** Add each card in your collection with its current market value, player name, sport, and era.

**Output:**
- Card-level exposure: each card as a % of total portfolio — red alert if over 20%
- Player-level exposure: all cards per player aggregated — red alert if over 30%
- Sport-level exposure: basketball vs. football vs. other — red alert if over 50%
- Era-level exposure: vintage / junk-wax / modern / ultra-modern — red alert if over 60%

**Summary dashboard** shows:
- Total portfolio value
- Current rule compliance status (pass/fail for all four rules)
- Largest concentration risks ranked by severity
- Rebalancing suggestions: which positions to reduce and by how much to return to compliance

**Scenario modeling tab:** Adjust any position's value to model market movements. See how a 60% crash in your largest position affects your overall portfolio health and which rules it breaks.

Designed for portfolios of 20–500 cards. Built for Google Sheets — no subscriptions, no login, no data sharing.
