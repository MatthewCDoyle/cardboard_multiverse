## What's in the kit

Five documents plus one spreadsheet, all designed to be used together at card shows:

---

**Document 1: Pre-Show Setup Checklist (PDF)**

A sequenced checklist for everything from packing the car to opening the table. Organized in three phases:

- Night before: inventory sort, pricing stickers, bag check
- Show morning: table layout diagram, signage placement, box labeling
- First 30 minutes live: opening giveaway prep, showcase final check, cash float setup

Includes a table layout diagram for a standard 6-foot and 8-foot table — showing optimal placement for bargain boxes, showcase, binder, and payment station.

---

**Document 2: Color-Coded Bargain Box Pricing Framework (PDF)**

The system explained in one laminate-ready page. Box color definitions, what goes in each tier, restocking trigger (when to refill), and the psychology of why the system works. Print one per table.

---

**Document 3: Showcase Rotation Log (PDF)**

A fill-in log for tracking what's in your showcase at show open, what sold, when it sold, and what you rotated in. Helps you identify your fastest-moving price points and best-performing card types across multiple shows.

Print a fresh copy for each show. After 5–10 shows, patterns emerge that tell you what to source more of.

---

**Document 4: Sourcing Script Reference Card (PDF)**

The one-more-question framework condensed to a pocket card. Includes exact language for estate sales, card show floor buying, and cold approaches to dealers with under-the-table inventory. Laminate and keep in your bag.

---

**Document 5: Post-Show Debrief Template (PDF)**

A one-page template to complete in the car or at home after every show:

- Total revenue vs. target
- Best-performing card type (by category and price tier)
- Worst-performing (sat all day)
- Inventory gaps to source before next show
- One thing to do differently next time

Sellers who debrief after every show improve faster than those who don't. This template takes 10 minutes and compounds over a year of shows.

---

**Spreadsheet: Daily Sales Log (Google Sheets)**

Track every transaction at the show in real time on your phone:

- Card sold
- Price tier (bargain box color or showcase price)
- Sale price
- Payment method (cash / Venmo / card)
- Running total revenue

Summary tab auto-calculates: total revenue, revenue by price tier, total transactions, average sale price, and cash vs. digital payment split.

$24. Instant download. Five PDFs + one Google Sheet.
