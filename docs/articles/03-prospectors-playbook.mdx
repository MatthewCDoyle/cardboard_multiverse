## Who this course is for

The Prospector archetype is rewarded enormously when right and punished severely when wrong. This course is for collectors who want to play the high-risk, high-reward prospecting game with a system rather than pure instinct.

## Module 1: The Prospector Archetype

An honest assessment of what prospecting actually delivers — the hit rate, the typical return on hits, the carrying cost of misses, and the mental model required to sustain it across a career. Many collectors think they want to be Prospectors but are actually better suited to other archetypes. This module helps you decide.

## Module 2: The Transcendent Scorecard System

The full framework: Age, Draft Pedigree, Persona, and Winning scored on a 1–3 scale. Detailed breakdowns of how to score each dimension with available pre-draft information. Historical calibration: what score did LeBron, Mahomes, and Wembanyama receive if you applied this scoring before their rookieyear card prices peaked? How accurate was the model?

## Module 3: Draft Season Intelligence

The mechanics of timing in the prospect market: when prices are cheapest (post-draft night, before first training camp), when the first spike occurs (first preseason games), and when the true value confirmation event happens (All-Star first selection, playoff performance). Each timing window has a different risk-reward profile.

## Module 4: Managing the Prospect Position

How to determine when to hold a prospect card versus sell at first confirmation. The common mistake of holding too long after the narrative peak. Position sizing: how much of your portfolio should be in any single prospect, and how does that change as the career develops?

**Deliverable:** Transcendent Scorecard Spreadsheet (player scoring template with historical calibration data)
